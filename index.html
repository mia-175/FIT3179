Human Development Index (HDI) 2022

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a summary measure of three key dimensions of human development: standard of living (income),
education and health.

These are measured by gross national income (GNI) per capita, mean years of schooling, and life expectancy at birth (respectively).

This visualisation aims to explore the key dimensions of human development of countries around the world; in particular the differences between affluent nations and those with limited economic resources.

The bottom three visualisations show the top and bottom 10 countries in each indicator.
